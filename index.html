About Nexus Logistics
OUR MISSION
Get freight professionals the intelligence they need — faster than anyone else.
Freight markets don't wait. Diesel spikes overnight. A geopolitical event closes a strait and rates double in a week. By the time most carriers and brokers hear about it, the window is already closing. Nexus Logistics delivers the intelligence that moves the needle — sourced daily, structured for action, and built to keep you ahead of the market instead of reacting to it.

Nexus Logistics is a daily freight intelligence platform built for carriers, brokers, and supply chain professionals. Every morning we pull current data from across the logistics landscape — rates, disruptions, fuel prices, trade policy, and market signals — and synthesize it into a single, structured briefing.

Every article, disruption, and market signal includes a real source link so you can verify and dig deeper. When major events are developing — a geopolitical crisis, a fuel spike, a regulatory change — the platform surfaces the intelligence you need before it hits the trade press headlines.

Coverage spans 9 verticals: ocean freight, air cargo, truckload, rail, ports, warehousing, trade policy, fuel markets, and supply chain risk.

HOW SCORES ARE CALCULATED
Every company in this database is scored 0–100 based on four factors that indicate how likely they are to be a strong, active freight customer right now. Higher scores mean more signals pointing to active freight procurement — not just company size.
42
Signal Strength (up to 42 pts)
Number of confirmed buying signals — job postings for logistics roles, earnings calls citing freight costs, capacity expansions, new facilities, and hiring activity. Each signal adds 7 points.
20
Intelligence Recency (up to 20 pts)
How recently the intelligence was verified. Updated within 24 hours earns full points; within 7 days earns 15; within 30 days earns 10. Older data scores 3 points.
15
Freight Type Specificity (up to 15 pts)
Specialized freight modes score higher because they require dedicated carrier relationships. Reefer, Hazmat, and Oversized earn 15 pts. Flatbed earns 12. Dry Van and others earn 8.
15
Source Quality (up to 15 pts)
Companies sourced from SEC filings, 10-K reports, or earnings calls earn full marks — these are verified financial disclosures. Multiple corroborating sources earn 10 pts. Single sources earn 5.
90–100 · High Priority — Multiple verified signals, active procurement, specialized freight need
80–89 · Moderate — Strong signals but fewer verified data points or lower freight complexity
Below 80 · Developing — Fewer signals or dry van only; worth monitoring but not immediate outreach
